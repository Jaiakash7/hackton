ADMINISRATION DESK

Mr.N.Sreekanth
Secretary

Meenakshi Sundararajan Engineering College (MSEC) was established by the IIET Society in 2001. This institution is a part of the prestigious KRS Group of Institutions which also includes the renowned IIET (Indian Institute of Engineering Technology) established in 1947 by our Founder Late Shri K.R.Sundararajan, the well-known Meenakshi College for Women and the more recently established Meenakshi Sundararaja School of Management. The institutions on the KRS Campus are known for the quality education they impart and their stringent levels of discipline. We have consistently outshone all our peers, not only in academics, but in co- curricular activities as well. Since inception MSEC has shown consistent performance in academics and extra-curricular spheres. The management strives constantly to develop this institution into a "CENTRE OF EXCELLENCE" imparting Engineering education to young men and women, grooming their overall personality with the highest emphasis on ethical values and honing them to face the challenges of the industry and the nation at large.

Dr. S. V. Saravanan
Principal

I am extremely delighted and honored to serve as the Principal of this esteemed institution. At Meenakshi Sundararajan Engineering College, we are committed to providing a holistic education that goes beyond academic excellence and aims at the overall development of every individual. Our college has a rich history of over 22 years. We have consistently strived for excellence in all aspects of education. We have a team of dedicated faculty members who are experts in their respective fields and are passionate about imparting knowledge to our students. They go the extra mile to ensure that our students not only excel in academics but also develop a strong value system and interpersonal skills. We believe in creating an inclusive and nurturing environment for our students, where they can explore their talents, experiment with new ideas, and develop a thirst for lifelong learning. We provide a wide range of academic programs at the undergraduate and postgraduate levels, enabling students to choose the field that aligns with their aspirations and interests. Our emphasis on practical learning and industry exposure equips our students with the necessary skills to excel in their chosen careers. Apart from academics, we encourage our students to actively participate in co- curricular and extracurricular activities, as we believe that a well-rounded individual is better prepared to face the challenges of the dynamic world. Our campus boasts state-of-the-art facilities, including well-equipped laboratories, libraries, sports amenities, and cultural spaces, providing students with ample opportunities to explore their talents and interests. We also strive to foster strong industry-academia collaborations, ensuring that our students gain practical insights and exposure to real-world scenarios. Our placement cell works tirelessly to connect our students with leading organizations and facilitate their entry into the professional world. At Meenakshi Sundararajan Engineering College, we take pride in our commitment to producing ethical, responsible, and socially conscious professionals who will contribute positively to society. We believe in providing a nurturing and disciplined environment that facilitates personal growth, fosters critical thinking, and promotes innovation.
